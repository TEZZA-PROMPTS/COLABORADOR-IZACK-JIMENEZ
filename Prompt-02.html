Seated upright at the table with one arm extended forward and the other raised holding a small cup. Torso slightly turned towards the camera, highlighting the structure of the upper body. Relaxed yet confident posture, conveying a casual, everyday moment.

HEAD POSITION AND POSITION
Head positioned straight with a slight downward tilt. Eyes directed straight at the camera, creating a strong and serene gaze. Neck naturally aligned with a relaxed posture.

Neutral and confident expression with relaxed facial muscles. Lips slightly closed with a slight natural volume. Composed, calm, and self-assured expression.

Short, dark hair with soft waves, well-trimmed and evenly distributed. Sharp, well-defined hairline. Natural, dense texture.

SKIN AND REALISM
Ultra-detailed skin rendering with visible pores, subtle highlights, and natural color variation. Warm sunlight creating a realistic skin response and a soft glow. Physically accurate subsurface scattering, typical of Blender Cycles rendering.

Modern lifestyle aesthetic — relaxed, confident, and subtly luxurious. Welcoming and cinematic atmosphere with editorial and social presence.

STYLE AND RENDERING QUALITY
Ultra-photorealistic rendering in Blender Cycles, cinematic realism, 8K resolution, HDR, extremely sharp focus, realistic micro-details of the skin, physically accurate lighting, editorial lifestyle quality, without stylization or artistic exaggeration.
